# Phase 3 — Strip the Styles

**Goal:** Remove all CSS and sit with the naked structure.

---

## What to Do

- Delete every stylesheet
- Look at what remains — pure HTML structure, component hierarchy, props
- Identify which styles were structural (layout, spacing) vs cosmetic (colour, shadow)

---

## Why This Matters

Most beginners treat styling and structure as one thing.
This phase proves they're separate layers. A good component tree should make sense without any CSS.

---
